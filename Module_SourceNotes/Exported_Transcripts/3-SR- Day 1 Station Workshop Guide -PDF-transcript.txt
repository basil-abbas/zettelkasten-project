STEP 2
Reflection 
Discuss these questions within your group:
Accountability vs. Stability
In Phase 2, you faced an ethical dilemma regarding leaders
taking extra supplies. If you choose to 'Tell the whole group,' you
risk a total collapse of trust and possible violence. If you 'Confront
them privately,' you might be seen as part of the cover-up later.
How does a leader balance the need for absolute transparency
with the need to keep the team stable and focused on a common
goal?

Reflection 
Discuss these questions within your group:
Strategic Alignment: Survival vs. Rescue
There are two distinct 'business goals' in this scenario: Operational
Stability (staying alive day-to-day) and Exit Strategy (getting
rescued). Which goal did your team prioritize in your Top 10? If a
team picks the Fishing Line but leaves the Signal Mirror, they are
investing in 'Operations' over 'Marketing/Visibility.' How does a
company balance keeping the lights on today versus investing in
its future escape or growth?
